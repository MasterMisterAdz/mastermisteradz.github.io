Empowering Communities

At the American Green Corner Sabah, we bring together technology, creativity, and education. With dedicated zones for innovation, collaboration, and relaxation, AGCS serves as a hub for learning, sustainability, and community engagement.

Explore Our Zones

Discover the six dynamic zones at AGCS:

  • Fab & Design Zone: Experience 3D printing and laser cutting.
  • Collab Zone: Work on projects with cutting-edge devices.
  • Technology Zone: Explore drones, robotics, and virtual reality.
  • Education USA: Access resources for studying in the USA.
  • Leisure Zone: Relax, play board games, or use touch-screen TVs.
  • Podcast Studio: Record professional-quality podcasts.
